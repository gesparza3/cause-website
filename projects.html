Wildfires

Given the increase in wildfire activity, we believe it is our responsibilty to raise awareness about how the average citizen can help reduce the chance of uncontrolled fires. We also hope to provide outreach to those who have been affected by the recent disasters. Since we live in an area with great wildfire potential we feel the Chico State family is directly connected to this topic.
