About CAUSE

Mission Statement

To present an educational awareness to students and the community about how to take action within a community and make a difference towards climate change and sustainability issues.

Objectives

  • This club will connect people in a civil way to community members to promote sustainable change
  • Take part in community sustainability projects and develop our own projects towards raising awareness
  • Educate students about the effects of climate change and what action there is to take in order make change
